Banana Milkshake Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. Milk
  2. Bananas
  3. Sugar
  4. Cashews
Ingredients Quantity
Bananas Two
Milk 1.5 cups

Steps to make banana milkshake:

  • Step1: Take two ripe bananas.cut them into small pieces. shift all the pieces to a blender.
  • Step2: Add cashews to a blender for a protein boost.
  • Step3: Add sugar as per the taste.
  • Step4: Add 1.5 cups of chilled milk to the blender.
  • Step5: Blend everything to smooth shake.
